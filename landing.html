From 1D boyband to solo artist and actor

Who is Harry Styles? How did he become the artist he is today?

Harry Edward Styles (01 February 1994) is an English singer, songwriter, and actor. His musical career began in 2010 as a part of the boy band One Direction, which went on to become one of the best-selling boy groups of all time before going on an indefinite hiatus in 2016.

Styles has released 3 albums through his solo-career: Harry Styles (2017), Fine Line (2019), and Harry's House (2022).

His film roles include Dunkirk (2017), Don't Worry Darling, and My Policeman (both 2022).

One Direction

1D is an english-Irish pop boy band formed in 2010 by Niall Horan, Liam Payne, Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson, and Zayn Malik.

One Direction's five albums, Up All Night (2011), Take Me Home (2012), Midnight Memories (2013), Four (2014), and Made in the A.M. (2015), topped charts in several countries, and generated hit singles including "What Makes You Beautiful", "Live While We're Young", "Best Song Ever", "Story of My Life" and "Drag Me Down".

Sadly, the band went on indefinite hiatus in January 2016, allowing all members to pursue other projects.
